Amines for Gas Treating

Our gas treating solvents are used in refineries and gas plants to improve safety , prevent corrosion and meet environmental regulations. This process is done by using aqueous solution of various amines to dissolve or remove hydrogen sulfide and carbon dioxide from the refinery sour gases giving us sweet gas.
