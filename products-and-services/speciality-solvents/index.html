Speciality Solvents

Speciality Solvents
for Carbon Capture & Storage

The carbon capture and storage or carbon sequestration is considered to be an important solution for long term clearance of C02 emissions. It is a known fact that CO2 is used in the oil and gas industry to have a better recovery of oil from a low quantity reservoir.

Even urea and ammonia plants the CO2 produced by the reforming process is recovered and fed to the urea synthesis section as a feedback. This process is excellent to reduce CO2 emissions. Amines have been used in many processes and are the most common product for reducing CO2 emissions from various gas streams.

Global chemicals offer Ion-Exchange and the Electrodialysis skid mounted unit for the removal of Heat Stable Salts which causes degenerative effects on the amines and the metallic surface of the machinery too. With our partners, we have developed a Specialty Amine, the AMINOSOL-FGS, which shows excellent resistance to oxygen degradation and minimum formation of HSS.
