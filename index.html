Global Chemical Company aims to bring the best of the world in providing unique chemical solutions with utmost safety, high quality and best practices. The company is one of the new age solution integrators having partnership with leading global chemical manufacturers - Brenntag and Amines & Plasticizers Limited (APLto provide best in class chemical solutions that are tailor made as per the clients’ needs. Brenntag is a global market leader in the chemical industry. With a far-reaching global network and complex supply chain management, we want the right products and services to reach the right consumer with ease. We truly believe in ConnectingChemistry for collaborative work with like minded people. Amines & Plasticizers Limited (APL) is a pioneer and largest producer of Ethanolamine, Alkyl Alkanolamines, Morpholine Derivatives like NMMO 50% & Gas Treating Solvents. Our focus is always on the Specialty Chemical Industry and to achieve global reorganization in this field. Becoming their partner for supply and manufacturing of specialty chemicals has opened many doors for Global Chemicals to reach new heights in business.
